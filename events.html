Past Events :

C-PROGRAMMING WORKSHOP [Oct-Nov 2019]

After completing this course, one should be able to read, write and solve problem of intermediate level using C programming. Our focus is to build understanding among students instead of cramming definitions.

TECHNOVOICE [Dec 2019-20]

It is a recruitment process of techknights club, in which there are three rounds to recruit the participants. After completing all three rounds the participant become the member of the club.

BLIND CODING [THAR 2K20]

It is a contest to check your creative approach about mark-up languages and accuracy to understand web pages. It is a totally based on basic markup language and also depend on designing skills of participants.


WEB-ZILLA [THAR 2K20]

It is a contest to check your creative approach about mark-up languages and accuracy to understand web pages. It is a totally based on basic markup language and also depend on designing skills of participants.

BUGGY BOX [THAR 2K20]

It will be based on finding vulnerabilities and loopholes in an Android application(Buggy Box) without any use of desktop and use them to flag off theirselves as winner . To make it fun for everyone, it will be based on logic, knowledge and experience excluding any programming needed.

GOOGLE HUNT ND EGIZMA [THAR 2K20]

It is a teo round event.First one is Google hunt in which one have to reach the destination using clues in Google map. Egizma is kbc based technical quiz.


THE FORBIDDEN FOREST [THAR 2K20]

It is a fun event with a touch of technology, in which he participant will have to reach the centre of a maze in limited time, but with a V.R. box on, so the maze won’t be visible. Participants will be shown the map of maze just before the game starts. They will have to find the path by their memory.

THE GAMING LEAGUE [THAR 2K20]

It will be a 2-day event in which participants will play three different games depending upon their interest. The games are very popular shooting and arcade racing games so almost everyone will be familiar with them.
